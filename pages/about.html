About Our Portal

The Campus Lost & Found Portal is a dedicated platform designed to help students and staff easily report, search, and recover lost belongings within the university premises.

Our goal is to provide a centralized, efficient, and user-friendly system where lost and found items can be managed effectively, ensuring that everything finds its way back to its rightful owner.

Our Features

Report Lost Items

Submit a detailed report including category, location, and date if you've lost an item on campus.

🙌

Report Found Items

🔍

Browse & Search

Easily filter and search through all reported items by keywords, categories, or lost/found status.
